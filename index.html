About

Strapazza is committed to serving authentic Italian food in a lively, homey atmosphere. Our menu features classic Italian cooking and incorporates the best and freshest seasonal produce, pasta, meat, and seafood. Strapazza serves hand-tossed pizzas, house-made pastas, gourmet salads, and more.
